# Inaccessible McCulloch Papers

Papers that could not be accessed in full text during this research. Listed with known publication details and access points.

## Not Found as Standalone Paper

### 6. "Redundancy of Potential Command"
- **Status:** No standalone paper with this exact title has been identified.
- **The concept** was developed across multiple papers and lectures in the 1950s-60s.
- **Published treatment:** Appears in McCulloch's work on the reticular formation (with Bill Kilmer) and is collected in *Embodiments of Mind*.
- **Secondary sources:** Stafford Beer, *Beyond Dispute* (Wiley, 1994), pp. 157-158.

### 7. Reliability of Nets from Unreliable Components
- **Status:** McCulloch's specific contributions on this topic are embedded in his broader work.
- **The most famous paper on this topic** is actually von Neumann's "Probabilistic Logics and the Synthesis of Reliable Organisms from Unreliable Components" (1956), which builds on McCulloch-Pitts.
- **Related:** Winograd & Cowan, *Reliable Computation in the Presence of Noise* (MIT Press, 1963); Cowan, "The Problem of Organismic Reliability" (1965).

## Recommendation

The most cost-effective approach: acquire *Embodiments of Mind* (MIT Press, ~$30). It contains the 1943, 1945, 1947, 1951, 1948, and 1959 papers, plus 15 others. That single book covers most of what's behind paywalls above.
