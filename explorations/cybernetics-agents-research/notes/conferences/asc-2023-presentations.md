# ASC 2023 — Monthly Presentation Series & Conference Activity

The ASC did not hold a standalone annual conference in 2023 (the 60th Anniversary in 2024 was the next major event). However, the society ran monthly presentations and participated in other conferences.

## 2023 Monthly Presentations

1. **"Feedback Loop(s) of Transdisciplinary Practice"** — October 29, 2023
2. **"NewMacy: Finding New Trails in Pursuit of Resilience"** — September 17, 2023
3. **"Sustainable Self-governance in business and societies — VSM in Action"** — May 21, 2023
4. **"Enacting Cybernetics"** — April 30, 2023
5. **"Language, Evolutionary Leadership and Conscious Evolution"** — February 19, 2023
6. **"Frictions, Fictions"** — January 22, 2023

## Conference Participations

### RSD12 (October 2023, Carnegie Mellon University)
- #NewMacy "Colloquies" sessions took place at CMU and online.
- Focused on cybernetic alternatives to current AI approaches.

### ISSS 2023 (June 17–23, 2023, Kruger National Park, South Africa)
- ASC participation in systems science discussions.

## Assessment

The ASC's 2023 activity was primarily its monthly presentation series and participation in other conferences (ISSS, RSD12). The "VSM in Action" and "Enacting Cybernetics" presentations are relevant to our project. The #NewMacy Colloquies at CMU represent the most sustained engagement with AI-related themes.
