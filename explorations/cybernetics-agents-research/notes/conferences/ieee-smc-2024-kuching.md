## Papers Found (from DBLP and IEEE Xplore)

### Multi-Agent / Adaptive Control
1. **"Dynamic Event-Triggered Distributed MPC for UAV-UGV Systems Against DoS Attacks"**
   - Authors: Hui Tang, Yong Chen
   - Distributed model predictive control for coordinated autonomous vehicles under communication threats.

2. **"Formation and Containment Fuzzy Control via Interval Type-2 Approach for Multiple Autonomous Ships"**
   - Authors: Yann-Horng Lin, Yi-Chen Lee, Wen-Jer Chang, Cheung-Chieh Ku, Chein-Chung Sun
   - Coordinated control for multiple autonomous maritime vessels.

3. **"Integrating Task Allocation and Hierarchical Reinforcement Learning for Optimized Cargo Transport Routing"**
   - IEEE Xplore doc 10831011
   - Multi-robot cargo transport in warehouses using hierarchical RL.
   - Division of labor among multiple robots — cybernetic themes of requisite variety in task allocation.

## Assessment

The conference covers the three pillars of the SMC society: Systems Science & Engineering, Human-Machine Systems, and Cybernetics. Multi-agent systems papers at SMC 2024 continue to focus on **distributed control under adversarial conditions** (DoS attacks, faults) — a practical engineering framing of Ashby's Law (maintaining requisite variety under perturbation).
