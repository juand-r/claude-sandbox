# Vahidi, Aliahmadi & Teimoury (2019) - Research Status and Trends of Management Cybernetics and VSM

**Citation:** Vahidi, A., Aliahmadi, A. and Teimoury, E. (2019), "Researches status and trends of management cybernetics and viable system model", *Kybernetes*, Vol. 48 No. 5, pp. 1011-1044.

**DOI:** 10.1108/K-11-2017-0433

**Affiliation:** Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST), Tehran

**Access:** Emerald Insight (paywalled). ResearchGate has preprint.

## Purpose

Reviews the underpinning principles and scientific trends of cybernetics and VSM. Aims to guide researchers and managers active in management cybernetics about past, current, and future trends.

## Method

- Mixed: qualitative historical review + quantitative frequency analysis of top 1,000 publications in management cybernetics
- Maps out the evolution from Macy conferences (1946) through Wiener, Ashby, Von Foerster, McCulloch, to Beer's management cybernetics

## Key Findings

- Management cybernetics = combination of system, control, and management sciences (Beer's contribution)
- **The main application area of VSM is in the IT field and large-scale organizations** -- directly relevant to our project
- Traces VSM development steps and identifies research gaps
- Key journals: Kybernetes, Systemic Practice and Action Research, Systems Research and Behavioral Science, JORS, IEEE, EJOR, Cybernetics and Systems

## Relevance to Our Project

This is an essential mapping paper. It confirms that IT/software is the primary application domain for VSM, which validates our intuition that cybernetic organizational models should translate well to software agent architectures. The bibliometric analysis provides a roadmap for further reading.
