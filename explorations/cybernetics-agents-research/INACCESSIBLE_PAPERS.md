# Papers We Could Not Access

## Beer, S. (1979). *The Heart of Enterprise*. Wiley.
- **Status:** Partial access. Full text behind controlled digital lending on Internet Archive (https://archive.org/details/heartofenterpris0000beer). Requires IA account + borrowing.
- **What we got:** Extensive secondary coverage of the formal apparatus (axioms, principles, theorem, law of cohesion). The formal elements are widely and consistently reproduced in the VSM literature.
- **What we missed:** Beer's own extended philosophical arguments, the full case studies (especially the mutual insurance company), the "Later in the bar" dialogues, and any subtleties in how he builds the argument across 582 pages.
- **Deep-dive notes:** `notes/deep-dives/beer-1979-heart-of-enterprise.md`

## Thompson, R. (2011). "Viable Computing Systems: A Set Theory Decomposition of Beer's VSM." PhD thesis, LJMU.
- **Status:** PDF at core.ac.uk returned 404. Listed at https://core.ac.uk/download/pdf/80684155.pdf
- **Relevance:** The most rigorous formal mathematical treatment of Beer's VSM found — uses set theory to decompose the model algebraically.
