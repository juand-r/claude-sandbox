# Student Notes on Chapter 9: "Entropy Rate and the Identity h = log(1/R)"

## Overall impression

This chapter makes an important and subtle distinction (counting GF vs length
PGF) that many sources conflate. The historical survey from Shannon through
Scheibner-Smith-Bialek is well-chosen. The "slow convergence as a diagnostic"
section is the highlight.
